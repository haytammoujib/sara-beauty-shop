HOW TO USE

Apply a few drops and massage into hair ends for shine, frizz control and hydration. As a mask treatment, apply a generous amount to clean, dry hair and leave in overnight; then rinse or wash out gently with regular shampoo. Can be used daily. Use at least 2-3 times a week and less frequently as hair’s health returns. For external use only.
